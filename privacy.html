Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the PortPocket app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Christopher De Jong (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Freemium service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect or transmit personal information to the Service Provider's servers. Your data stays on your device, and some data may optionally be stored in your iCloud account if iCloud syncing is enabled.

Data Stored on Your Device

The Application stores data you enter (such as job entries and related details) locally on your device to provide core functionality.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If iCloud syncing is enabled, your app data is stored in your iCloud account using Apple CloudKit. The Service Provider does not have access to your iCloud data. Apple processes this data under Apple's privacy policies.

Location Data

The Application may access your device's location to provide mapping and navigation features. Location is used on-device to:

  • Map Services: Show your position on terminal maps and calculate distances to terminals
  • Navigation Features: Provide navigation assistance and directional indicators
  • Enhanced User Experience: Surface relevant terminal information based on proximity

Location data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to the Service Provider.

Camera and Photos

The Application may access your camera or photo library to capture and manage job ticket images. Photos remain on your device unless you choose to store them in your own iCloud Photos. The Service Provider does not receive these images.

Motion and Compass

The Application may use motion and compass data to improve navigation and orientation on maps. This data is processed on-device.

Push Notifications

If you enable notifications, Apple delivers notifications to your device. The Service Provider does not receive personal data through this process beyond basic delivery information required by Apple.

Subscription Information

The Application offers premium subscriptions processed through Apple's App Store. The Service Provider receives confirmation of your subscription status from Apple but does not have access to your payment information, which is handled entirely by Apple.

Third Party Access

The Service Provider does not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Apple App Store: For subscription processing and app distribution
  • Apple iCloud/CloudKit: If you enable iCloud syncing
  • Legal Requirements: As required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process
  • Safety: When we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect rights, safety, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request

Tracking

The Application does not use tracking technologies to link your data across apps or websites and does not sell your personal data.

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling it. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.

You can disable location, camera, photos, motion, and notification access at any time through your device's settings.

Data Retention Policy

App data you enter is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the Application. If you use iCloud syncing, data is retained in your iCloud account until you delete it there.

The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.
