‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I navigate to a terminal?

1. Browse or search for your terminal
2. Tap the terminal name
3. Tap "Open in Maps" to get directions in Apple Maps

Can I search for specific terminals?

Yes! Use the search bar at the top to find terminals by name or company. You can also filter by port (LA or Long Beach).

Why does the app need location permission?

Location is optional and only used to show your position on the map. The app works perfectly without it - you can still view all terminals and get directions.

Which terminals are included?

Port of Los Angeles: APM Terminals, Fenix Marine Services, TraPac, Yusen, China Shipping, Everport, West Basin

Port of Long Beach: LBCT, TTI, SSA Marine, ITS, SSA Terminals, PCT, OOCL

Is the app free?

Yes, Port Navigator is completely free with no ads or in-app purchases.
